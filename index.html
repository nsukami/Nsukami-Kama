About Me

Every brand has stories to tell—stories that will not only engage, inform, surprise, delight, and impact their audience, but that will also deliver on measurable business goals. I work to tell these stories through my love for learning, training and empowering people to bridge the gap between the brand and the client. Now, I use my current role as a Product Advisor to advise clients on technology and help provide them with solutions through hardware or software that lets them do more.
